Our Simple 5-Step Conveyancing Process

1

Quote & Engagement

Receive your instant fixed-fee quote online or over the phone. Sign engagement letter digitally in under 2 minutes.

2

Contract Review & Advice

We review your contract and provide plain-English advice on special conditions, inclusions, and potential issues.

3

Searches & Due Diligence

We order and analyse all required searches (council, water, land tax, owners corporation, etc).

4

Pre-Settlement Preparation

Adjustments calculated, transfer documents prepared, stamp duty & lodgement with SRO and Titles Office.

5

Settlement Day

Electronic settlement via PEXA. Funds transferred, keys released. You receive confirmation within minutes.
